Testimonials

AvidBiotics Corporation is a San Francisco Bay area biotherapeutics company deploying proprietary, biotechnology-based platforms for multiple applications in life sciences with a business plan that is unconventional and austere. With 100% of the employees with scientific credentials and dedicated to research and development the company needed an IT consulting partner that could perform many IT technical consulting and prompt customer service allowing us to maintain more management focus to our core research and business.

With virtually no internal IT expertise AvidBiotics Corporation consequently decided to secure a relationship with a proven IT hosting and consulting company who could advise us on multiple technical decisions; help us reduce cost; reduce the management focus required to manage IT; and provide an on-going level of consulting and technology innovation to our operation.

For our goals and needs, Assured Technology was the right choice. Brian James, CEO of Assured Technology, and his team worked closely with us to ensure our projects and processes were achieved efficiently, cost-effectively and on-time and on-budget.

Reflecting on our relationship, it is clear we achieved our stated IT consulting goals in the most value-oriented way possible. We have achieved the on-time and on-budget imperatives we desired and have enjoyed a relationship with a company that is very service oriented and responsive. We would encourage companies with a similar set of business needs to investigate working with Assured Technology.

Jim Knighton
President
AvidBiotics Corporation
